The 59th

What is the 59th?

At the beginning of the quarantine in 2020, I embarked on many walks throughout the neighbourhood I grew up in. It became a small project that my friends and I adoringly named "The 59th", as that's the street number I'm on. The purpose of this collection is to showcase the small pieces and indications of livelihood even during a global pandemic; it also aligns with my overall goal to showcase the small things in life.
