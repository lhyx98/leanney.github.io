about me

Brene Brown wrote in her book, "the only unique contribution that we will ever make in the world will be born out of our creativity.‚Äù This quote fundamentally shifted the way I looked at the multitude creative passions I had growing up. A couple mongths after reading this book, the quarantine hit and I found myself relying on the arts to keep myself entertained.

I started my journey in photography with a Canon Rebel T2i. Over time, my interest, passion, and pursuit of photography has waxed and waned. However my purpose has remained the same: to capture moments to remember. Through my photography, I aim to inspire confidence and connection in my portraits, and bring a sense of joy or tranquility in my other work. I want to showcase the beauty of the mundane so that more people can appreciate the beauty in simplicity.

Professionally, I am pursuing a degree in Commerce with a major in Operations Management. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, playing piano, and learning new things (like coding)!
